Basic PC Maintenance (Windows 7)

Lesson Description

  • Click on start button and go to control panel
  • Click on system and security
  • Go down to administrative tools
  • There will be two tools you will be using: Free up disk space & defragment your hard drive
  • Click on free up disk space first, wait while it is calculating, then and check the files you want to delete
  • Click ok and select delete files
  • Next go back under administrative tools and click on defragment your hard drive
  • Then select your hard drive then click on defragment disk
  • Be patient and wait for it to be done
  • Close all windows
