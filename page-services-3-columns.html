Jerin Bryant

Autobiography

Jerin Bryant was born and raised on the southside of Chicago. She has 4 brothers and is the only girl amongst her siblings. Jerin grew up in a single parent home with her mother who was always working to make sure her children had everything. Jerin’s mother is her role model because her mother worked very hard and never gave up. In school, Jerin always got A’s and was an AP and honors student. She was also a cheerleader inside and outside of school, a spokesperson for the student council, and a part of female mentoring. As a very involved student, Jerin still managed to get good grades and graduate from CICS Longwood Academy high school at #5 of 200 in 2013. Jerin continued her education right after high school in the fall to Illinois state university. She spent 2 years there pursuing different interest in majors. After two years, Jerin was still unsure on what major to go into and was ready for a new school or opportunity. Year up became that new opportunity to take on something totally different along with an internship opportunity. Taking on a software track, Jerin has made a long strive to learn and obtain the skills to become successful in the corporate world. Jerin’s short term goals are to have a successful internship and graduate year up. Her long term goals are to start a career from her internship opportunity or to become a TA at year up for software. Eventually, jerin would like to also finish her last year of college to obtain a bachelor’s degree.
