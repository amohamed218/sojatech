How to make an Effective PowerPoint

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will learn a few tips & tricks to an effective powerpoint and better presentation skills. Some tips & tricks to an effective powerpoint:

  • Choose a theme that is appropriate for the topic and the audience
  • No paragraphs. Use the 7x7 rule meaning no more than 7 lines with 7 words per line.
  • Use pictures and graphs that are relevant to your topic
  • Practice giving your presentation
  • Be confident
  • Use projection when giving your presentation to ensure that everyone hears you in a large or small room.
  • Engage with the audience by using eye contact and gestures
  • It is okay to read part of your powerpoint, but make sure you know to look at the crowd. It is important to know your presentation before hand so you are not reading directly from the slides.
  • Use note cards if necessary; note cards are helpful when you need help remembering the content. Be sure to not write your whole presentation on the note cards, only include keywords to trigger your memory for your presentation.
  • Ask for feedback
