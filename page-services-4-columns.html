Biography

Omar Sharif was born and raised in West Africa, Ghana. He has a knack for learning a lot about technology and utilizing it the right way especially in computer hardware and also a little bit of software. Currently, he is a student at Year up Chicago who is working his way through this program to develop his communication and technology skills. One of his short term goals is to graduate from this program and use the necessary skills to help him attain his A+ certification in computer hardware. Another short term goal is to earn a degree in Computer Science and also increase his speed and accuracy to think critically and solve hard problems he may encounter on his journey thus making him a respectable asset in the corporate world. His long term goal is to create his own company and be able to use other people with different thoughts on how technology works. During his leisure time, Omar loves to play soccer, basketball, and hang out with the right friends and read. He is a type of person who engages himself among positive minded people and also tries hard to emulate others who have done better and succeeded in the career path he's headed as a computer technician. His enthusiasm and ability to strive hard and learn more makes him a person with goals to fulfill and also make his parents proud. However, this program is shaping and setting him up for success.
